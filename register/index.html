Rules and guidelines regarding the event:

Registration
  1. By registering and making a payment you agree that all details provided are accurate. Any discrepancy may result in cancellation of registration.
  2. Cancellation and rescheduling of registration are not allowed.
  3. All tickets are non-refundable, non-transferable and is valid for one attendee only.
  4. Holder of any duplicate or forged passes/badges/tickets will be handed over to the security and/or police personnel. Forging passes is a criminal offence.

Venue
  1. We urge you to mark your presence at the meeting 15 minutes prior to event timing to avoid on time joining issues
  2. All attendees must have their ticket-id (which will be assigned and sent on your mail)
  3. Entry into the meeting room will only start 30 minutes before the event timing.
  4. The meeting room doors will remain closed during sessions and will not open for entry by .
  5. Attendees reaching late at the venue will not be allowed inside the auditorium while the sessions are in progress.
Solicitation
  1. The organisers reserve the rights of frisk and restrict entry.
  2. Any individual might be asked to leave the venue without any liability of a refund if there is any behavioural misdemeanour. This decision rests with the conference and venue management and is final and binding.
  3. There will be adequate breaks for networking.
  4. All standard virtual venue and attendee rules and/or mannerisms are to be adhered to by attendees.

Socialise
  1. Use the official hashtag #tedxycce on social media to talk about the event.
  2. Most importantly, have a great time!
