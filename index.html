About the event

We all are aware of the need for stairs, may it be for climbing on the terrace or those invisible ones needed to climb to your destiny. Same comes when we imagine for a society of dreams. Each day, continuous efforts, trying to get one step ahead than we were on yesterday, to enjoy the prettiest view of accomplishment.

Every single step you take needs a lot of courage, unending efforts, awareness, innovation, management, knowing the proper direction to walk into an immense passion to achieve it. To unwrap the invisible foundation of the stairs and explore its incredible power, join us at TEDxYCCE 2021 with our broad theme “Under the Stairs” which is an idea to uncover the hidden treasure below the Stairs.

About TEDx


In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program for local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded with TEDx, where x = an independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.
