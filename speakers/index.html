Speakers List for TEDxYCCE 2021

Onkar Khullar

Author | Philosoper | Blogger

Onkar Kishan Khullar is a 3-time college dropout turned motivational speaker, artist, author, entrepreneur of a social impact laboratory called I Impact India Partners which provides comprehensive social sector solutions to Non-Profits, Social Enterprises, and Corporates. He has given 17 TEDx talks till date and has done over 500 shows across India. He is often said as India's most dangerous speaker.

Kunal Moon

Artist | Consultant | Aggregator

Kunal Moon, from Mumbai, Maharashtra, has been a creative mind from early student days. He is a B. Tech. in Computer Science and an alumnus of IIM Calcutta.The self taught artist brings a style of abstraction and distortion in depicting the Indian classical dance forms in this present series. His experience of over 10 years is visible in each of his painting in this series aesthetically engaging in its own vibrant colours with harmony. He also brings in a sense of intrigue due to fragmentation of the subject being a novelty and uniqueness of his style.

Adv. Sidharth Arora

Educationist | Advocate | Author

Dr. Siddharth Arora is a multi talented personality with a degree of M.B.B.S, L.L.B and working as advocate at Supreme Court of India. What is more striking is the difference he has made as an educator. Dr. Arora truly believes that if he can change at least one life with his teaching, he would have done his job. He has not only shaped the journeys, but also the minds of many.

Mrs. Shivani Sharma Singh

Connoisseur | Content creator | Speaker

Mrs Shivani Singh Sharma has represented India internationally and has been crowned as Mrs Asia Pacific Exquisite international 2017 in a glorious ceremony in Baltimore USA. She is the perfect example of women empowerment with her exceptional work she did to bring change to society. She is a brand ambassador, a purpose coach and a mother awarded the Prestigious Patron of the Planet Award in Global Innovation Exchange in 2019, Dr Sarojini Naidu Nightingale of the Nation Award 2017 and Dr Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan Award 2020. At present, she is working on her initiative of spreading Self Love along with Self Improvement. She believes that If She Can Do it, Anyone Can Do! She is best suited for the quote “A girl should be two things : who and what she wants.”

Dr. Sapna Sharma

Counselor | Motivational Speaker | Life coach

A Spiritual Counselor, Motivational Speaker, Life Coach & Trainer, Dr. Sapna Sharma qualified as an ophthalmic surgeon, and after professionally practicing for 10 years she went on to explore her calling in Training and Counselling.
Equipped with a plethora of Qualifications in Counseling, Psychotherapy, Life Coaching, Human Resources and Personnel Management, and resonating a strong belief in the potential of every living being. She has been a motivator of thousands in Central India.

Mr. Paritosh Anand

YouTube filmmaker | Author | Entrepreneur

Mr Paritosh Anand is well known as The Same Old Kid With A Dream’, all over the world is a YouTuber filmmaker, entrepreneur and author in the making, his first book titled “The Importance of Dreams” which is soon to be out, Paritosh holds two businesses of We Smile Media and Dream of the Defence, to his name just at the age of 21, while catering to his 200k+ audience across various platforms and have excellent achievements in the educational field too. He also co-hosts Top Ranking Podcasts. Paritosh Anand rightly perches -" A dream does not become reality through magic; it takes sweat, determination, and hard work."
